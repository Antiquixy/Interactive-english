Chapter 05

A new kind of intelligence,
arriving very quickly.

Generative AI can now write, draw, code, and convincingly imitate a human voice. The capabilities are remarkable. The pace is unprecedented. This could be AI..

01 · Generation

Generative AI

Systems can produce text, images, music and code in seconds — useful for creativity, and difficult to verify.

02 · Identity

Voice cloning & deepfakes

A few seconds of audio is enough to imitate a person convincingly, raising questions about consent and evidence.

03 · Labour

Automation

Tasks once performed by people are now performed by software, reshaping which skills are valued and how.

04 · Security

Cyber & misuse

The same systems that help defenders can also assist attackers, making safeguards a moving target.

05 · Privacy

Data & consent

Models are trained on vast amounts of human output, often gathered without explicit, informed consent.

06 · Governance

Safety & regulation

Because advanced AI now significantly impacts everyday society, governments, tech companies, and researchers are implementing mandatory safeguards, content tracing, and legal regulations.

The goal of this chapter is not fear. It is reflection. Technology is a series of decisions, made by people, that we can still meaningfully shape.

"It has become appallingly obvious
that our technology has exceeded our humanity."
— often attributed to Albert Einstein

Humanity created technology to improve life.

Technology now shapes how we think, communicate, learn and interact.

Artificial intelligence continues to evolve at extraordinary speed.

The challenge is no longer whether technology is powerful.

The challenge is whether we can develop the wisdom, the ethics, and the governance to guide it.
